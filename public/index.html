      Props-off verification

      1. Remove all propellers and place the drone on a stable surface.
      2. Connect and acquire controls, then run the latency test.
      3. Press and release every direction. Confirm the server-applied command changes to the expected single axis and returns to zero.
      4. Hold a direction and press STOP. Confirm STOP RTT appears and the server-applied command immediately returns to all zeros.
      5. With motors running and propellers still removed, hold a direction and close the browser tab. The server watchdog should stop movement within 250 ms.
      6. Test Land and Emergency separately. Emergency must cut the motors immediately.

      Flight controls

      Press and hold a direction. Keyboard: W/S pitch, A/D roll, Q/E yaw, R/F rise/fall.

      STOP, Space, or Escape immediately cancels movement. Land performs a controlled landing. Emergency cuts the motors.
